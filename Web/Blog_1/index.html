RE:LOST - ARC ONE SYNOPSIS

Toshiro, a Longhart College student, with formidable forbidden magic, is to be executed immediately after capture, as forbidden magic is, as it's name suggest, forbidden in the Eventide Realm.

About to give himself in, rumours about his sister being guillotined got to him. His fury got to his head, which lead to his huge tantrum, accidentally causing buildings worth millions of coins crumbling to the ground.

Being able to retrieve his sister, they accidentally enter another domain known as Japan, city of the forbidden.

Fortunately or not, they meet Mayu, a young chemist who takes them into her home, a place they never should have set their foots in.

Will our brave Toshiro meet his demise at the hands of the parliament, or suffer something far greater than death?
